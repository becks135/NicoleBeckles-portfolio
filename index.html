About me.

After spending over 10 years in the financial industry as an actuary, I decided to make a career shift and return to the tech world. While I loved the analytical challenges of helping companies assess their risk, I often felt like something was missing. I found that something when, on a whim, I decided to complete an online web development course. That course re-ignited a spark, and I remembered how much I loved to code. Since then, it has been full steam ahead.

As a web developer, I get excited about solving complex problems and figuring out what makes things tick - whether it's building accessible websites for various clients, or simply tinkering away on my own side projects.

No matter what challenges come my way, I am adaptable in both my professional life and personal pursuits. As an island gal, born and raised in Barbados, I love warm weather and going the beach.
